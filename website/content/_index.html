SIG Contributor Strategy is responsible for contributor experience, sustainability, governance, and openness guidance to help CNCF community groups and projects with their own contributor strategies for a healthy project.

{{< blocks/link-down color="info" >}}
{{< /blocks/cover >}} {{% blocks/lead color="primary" %}} The mission of CNCF SIG Contributor Strategy is to collaborate on strategies related to building, scaling, and retaining contributor communities, including (people) governance, communications, operations, and tools. We want to help grow flourishing, sustainable communities with smooth journeys throughout their CNCF project lifecycle. To do that we will: {{% /blocks/lead %}}
{{< blocks/section color="primary" >}} {{% blocks/feature %}} **Create intentional space** Host a maintainers circle omprised of those interested in growing their projects and joining fellow maintainers in related cross project discussions. {{% /blocks/feature %}} {{% blocks/feature %}} **Listen and Advise** Create informational and training resources including guides, tutorials and templates of best practices, trade-offs, strategies, building and participating in scalable contributor communities. {{% /blocks/feature %}} {{< /blocks/section >}} {{< blocks/section color="primary" >}} {{% blocks/feature %}} **Evaluate and Foster** Assist the TOC with assessments and due diligence of prospective new projects by developing community graduation criteria check points for rolling feedback and guidance. {{% /blocks/feature %}} {{% blocks/feature %}} **Educate and Engage** Provide guidance to end users on how to engage with contributors and vice versa. {{% /blocks/feature %}} {{< /blocks/section >}}
